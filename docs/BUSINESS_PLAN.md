# CashLens Business Plan & Strategy

## 1. Executive Summary

CashLens is a mobile-first personal finance management tool designed to bridge the gap between manual entry and automated tracking. By leveraging on-device OCR (ML Kit), CashLens allows users to track digital payments (QRIS/Banking screenshots) and physical receipts instantly, ensuring 100% financial visibility with zero manual effort.

## 2. Problem Statement

Many personal finance apps either require tedious manual input or invasive bank API integrations. In regions like Indonesia, QRIS and bank transfers are dominant, but data is often trapped in static screenshots or physical receipts.

## 3. Solution

- **Zero-Invasive Automation**: Uses OCR to read data from screenshots and receipts without needing bank credentials.
- **Privacy-First**: On-device processing ensures financial data remains with the user.
- **Multi-Currency & Cloud Sync**: Supports modern nomads and cross-border transactions.

## 4. Target Market

- **Millennials & Gen Z**: High adoption of QRIS and mobile banking.
- **Small Business Owners**: Need to track business vs. personal expenses on the go.
- **Digital Nomads**: Require multi-currency support and cloud backup.

## 5. Monetization Strategy (Freemium Model)

### Free Tier

- Manual transaction entry.
- Basic receipt scanning (up to 5 scans/month).
- Local storage only.
- Basic analytics.

### Premium Tier (Subscription/One-time Purchase)

- **Unlimited Scanning**: No limits on OCR processing.
- **Cloud Sync**: Secure backup via Supabase.
- **Advanced Analytics**: Detailed spending trends and exportable reports (CSV/PDF).
- **Customization**: Exclusive app icons, themes, and category icons.
- **Budgeting Alerts**: Real-time notifications when reaching budget limits.

## 6. Marketing & Growth

- **Portfolio Showcase**: Use as a flagship project for high-end engineering roles.
- **Social Sharing**: "Share your monthly savings" visual summaries.
- **Referral Program**: Invite a friend to unlock 1 month of Premium.
